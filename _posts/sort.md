### Go

```go
import "sort"

strs := []string{"c", "a", "b"}
sort.Strings(strs)
fmt.Println("Strings:", strs)

ints := []int{7, 2, 4}
sort.Ints(ints)
fmt.Println("Ints:   ", ints)
```
